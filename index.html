Welcome to the BMCC Academic Degree Map Builder

Academic degree maps outline course sequencing and provide students a visual roadmap whilst on their quest to complete the degrees offered at BMCC.

This tool is designed to help you visualize your academic progress and plan your degree. You can search for courses, add them to your degree map, and organize them by semester. You can also export your degree map as a PDF and share it with your advisor. This is a student-made resource and in no way is meant to replace official academic advising.
